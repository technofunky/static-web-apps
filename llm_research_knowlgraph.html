📖 Glossary

PPO
Clipped policy updates + critic network; stable RL.
DPO
Direct preference optimization via log‑probability ratios.
GRPO
Group‑relative advantage, no critic (DeepSeek).
CoT
Step‑by‑step reasoning prompting.
ToT
Tree search over reasoning steps.
RLHF
Human feedback reward model + PPO.
